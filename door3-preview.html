What this preview is — and what it isn't.

It is the working-draft Door 3 surface. The data is real, sourced from the 4 May 2026 full audit of every queryable barcode in the I500 catalogue against the Open Food Facts API. Filter labels use Brand owner rather than Competitor — the buyer context is named on the surface, not implied.

It isn't the bank-ready Tier 1-3 institutional version. Three additions remain queued before that:

  • Condition-lens chips (diabetes / hypertension / oral health / family) once the basket-aggregate scoring federation extension lands.
  • Decision Record overlay (per-SKU put-back ratio) once pilot decision-volume reaches the publication threshold.
  • Source-priority adjudication surfaced per row.

Refresh discipline: published headline figure shifts ≥1 percentage-point in either direction trigger a refresh. Every new SKU added to the catalogue triggers an Open Food Facts API check within 7 days.
