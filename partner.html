Institutional enquiries

A partner programme is a future layer.

SCANSMART is early — four months in, pre-funding, and focused on three things: the I500 data, securing funding, and landing a first NHS pilot. A formal partner programme is something we’ll build once there’s a proven pilot behind it, not before. We’d rather be straight about that than promote an offer that doesn’t exist yet.

What’s real right now.

The work institutions tend to ask about — borough-level audits, commissioned research, food-literacy curriculum, an NHS HealthStore pathway — is where we’re heading, not where we are today. What exists now is the I500 verified-product dataset that underwrites everything we publish, and an early pilot conversation we’re pursuing with Lambeth and the Health Innovation Network.

If you’re an NHS commissioner, council, GP network, school, community organisation, foundation, or research group and you can see a fit, we’d genuinely like to hear from you. Early interest helps shape what the programme eventually becomes.

Get in touch

Tell us what you’re working on.

Send a line about your programme and where you think there could be a fit. We’ll come back with an honest read on whether — and when — we could work together. Realistic response time: 3–5 business days. You can also email Admin@scansmart.uk directly.
