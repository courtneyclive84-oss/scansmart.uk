How this was put together

Methodology and sources.

Full methodology: scansmart.uk/methodology (linked above; reused across every Door 4 piece). Ranking by retailer best-seller tag and trade-press market position. FSA per-100g and per-portion thresholds applied. Teaspoons (4g sugar = 1 tsp) and salt sachets (0.5g salt = 1 sachet) as the visual conventions. Capture-date stamped on every figure: Monday 1 June 2026, 09:00 BST.

Sources: Open Food Facts product pages (per row in the table above); Tesco / Sainsbury’s / Aldi online category listings; British Baker — Sainsbury’s top-10 plant-bread leaderboard and Kantar UK bread value-growth references carried in The Grocer; FSA published thresholds for traffic-light calculation. No Wikipedia citations per institutional-grade discipline.

Unverified rows flagged honestly. Three rows fully verified with label data; seven rows named per best-seller signal but label data NOT transcribed in this audit pass — not estimated.
