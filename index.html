CheckIT

Scan any barcode. CheckIT shows you what’s really inside in plain language — teaspoons of sugar, sachets of salt, traffic-light verdict in seconds. Pick a lifestyle preference (sugar / salt / caffeine) or just General. Free. Works offline. The open space where everyone starts.
