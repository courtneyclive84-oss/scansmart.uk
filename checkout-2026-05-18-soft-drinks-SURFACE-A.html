How this was put together

Methodology and sources.

Full methodology: scansmart.uk/methodology (linked above; reused across every Door 4 piece). Ranking by retailer best-seller tag and trade-press market position. FSA per-100ml and per-portion drinks thresholds applied. Teaspoons (4g sugar = 1 tsp) and salt sachets (0.5g salt = 1 sachet) as the visual conventions. Capture-date stamped on every figure: Monday 18 May 2026, 09:00 BST.

Sources: Open Food Facts product pages (per row in the table above); Tesco / Sainsbury’s / Aldi online category listings; Grocer / NIQ Britain’s Biggest Brands 2026 Top 100; Statista 2024 UK soft-drink convenience-channel sales-value ranking; FSA published thresholds for traffic-light calculation.
